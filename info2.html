How is Virtual Reality Useful?

Virtual Reality has been used in the medical field since the 1990s, but thanks to advances in technology and the spread of commercial VR headsets, the capabilities of VR systems has never been stronger.

VR Applications in Medicine

Current examples of Virtual Reality being used in the medical field include surgical training, mental and physical therapy, as well as imaging solutions.
